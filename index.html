I'm a Computer System Researcher aiming at solid system research.

I’m now Assistant Professor at Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). My current research interest include big data stream processing system on emerging hardware, and system for emerging ML/AI applications (e.g., data stream mining, continuous AI, and federated learning).

Research Fellow/Research Assistant/Interns positions available. Please send me your CV for a discussion.

Prospective Students/Collaborators

I lead the intellistream team. Our vision is to enable fast streaming processes at every aspect of big data analytics: mining, learning, inferring, storing, querying and so on. We are hiring for Research Fellow/Research Assistant/Interns/Visiting Students. Feel free to join our slack channel for a discussion.

I’m looking for students/collaborators to work on the following research prototypes: AlianceDB (Very Efficient Stream Processing Operations on Modern Hardware), TStream (State-of-the-art Transactional Stream Processing System on Multicore CPUs). The general research topics include query parser and compiler, hardware-conscious system optimisation, and , parallel and distributed online machine learning/stream data mining.

You may propose related topics but they should be related to the topic of stream processing . My apologise that I may not response to requests if the proposals are out of my interests.
